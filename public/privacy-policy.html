Privacy Policy for Product Tracker

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Product Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Data Collection and Storage

Product Tracker is designed with privacy and HIPAA compliance in mind. All data entered into the App, including patient references, product details, and facility information, is stored locally on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption.

We do not transmit, collect, or store your data on external servers. You maintain complete control and ownership of your data.

3. Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including PIN-based authentication and inactivity timeouts. However, the ultimate security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security features (e.g., Face ID, Touch ID, or strong passcodes) in addition to the App's lock screen.

4. Audit Logging

The App maintains a local audit log of security events (such as logins, lockouts, and data modifications) to assist with compliance requirements. This log is also stored locally and encrypted.
